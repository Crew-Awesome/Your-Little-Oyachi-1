About the room

Slow, soft, and intentional.

“Your Little Oyachi” is a small, cozy pet room built as a way to spend a thoughtful minute with Oyachi. It started as a surprise for a special someone, and now it’s a little corner on the web where you can drop in, give a pat, and listen to how she reacts.

Back to home Play now

This experience is intentionally minimal. No clutter, no busy HUDs — just Oyachi, her toys, and the room she drifts around in. Tap or click to pet her; throw the ball when you want to keep her curious; and let her settle down whenever she wants a nap.

  • Built with PixiJS, so the canvas stays smooth on desktop and mobile.
  • Hand-crafted moments: pets, toys, costumes, and the sleepy reactions.
  • Updates arriving slowly with more interactions and world-building.
