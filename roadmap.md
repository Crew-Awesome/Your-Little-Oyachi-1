# Your little Oyachi — Feature Roadmap

## Phase 1 — Expressions & Emotional States
**What it adds**
- Embarrassed / shy face expression.
- Sleepy half-eyes idle expression.
- Falling asleep after inactivity.
- Wake-up animation on click/tap.

**Status**
- ✅ Sleep system (idle tired → sleep loop → wake on click/ball) is complete.

**Why it fits the game**
- Deepens the calm, affectionate personality without adding systems or obligations.
- Keeps interactions subtle and optional while enriching idle behavior.

**Risks to watch for**
- Avoid turning sleep into a timer-based requirement.
- Ensure wake-up remains gentle and optional.

## Phase 2 — Petting Depth
**What it adds**
- Short click = squish reaction.
- Click-and-hold = petting interaction.
- Petting audio (rubbing, purr).
- Different reactions based on pet type (gentle / excited).

**Status**
- ✅ Pet hold reactions (gentle + long-hold override) are complete.

**Why it fits the game**
- Reinforces affectionate interaction as the main mechanic.
- Adds tactile feedback without introducing progression or pressure.

**Risks to watch for**
- Avoid expanding petting into a scoring or reward system.
- Keep animations grounded and floor-based.

## Phase 3 — Cosmetics
**What it adds**
- Costume variants (bow, dress, outfits).
- Unlocks via discoveries (not currency).
- Optional toggles for visuals.

**Why it fits the game**
- Supports personalization without monetization or grind.
- Keeps the focus on expression and charm.

**Risks to watch for**
- Do not introduce currency, shops, or daily rewards.
- Ensure toggles remain optional and unobtrusive.

## Phase 4 — Toys
**What it adds**
- Ball toy (drag / toss).
- Oyachi follows the ball.
- Ball can wake her from sleep.

**Status**
- ✅ Basic ball toy with drag/toss, damping, and wake-on-collision is complete.
- ⏳ TODO: Add subtle spin/roll visuals.
- ⏳ TODO: Add soft idle bounce when resting.

**Why it fits the game**
- Adds a light, playful interaction that remains optional.
- Encourages gentle engagement without management mechanics.

**Risks to watch for**
- Avoid complex physics or chore-like gameplay.
- Keep behavior subtle and calming.

## Phase 5 — Achievements / Discoveries
**What it adds**
- Discovery-based achievements.
- In-game soft notifications.
- Achievement page below the game.

**Why it fits the game**
- Celebrates gentle exploration without pressure.
- Provides a quiet sense of acknowledgement, not progression.

**Risks to watch for**
- No visible XP bars or numeric progression.
- Avoid turning discoveries into a grind.

## Optional / High-risk Ideas
**Hidden XP or memory system (no UI)**
- **What it adds:** Invisible memory of interactions to subtly influence expressions.
- **Why it fits:** Can deepen personality without visible metrics.
- **Risks:** Must remain fully hidden and never imply progression.

**Time-of-day flavor**
- **What it adds:** Small expression shifts based on local time.
- **Why it fits:** Adds ambience without new systems.
- **Risks:** Must not create schedules or expectations for the player.
